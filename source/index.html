Case Study: Lions Laser vision

In my last role (Creative Director) at Digital agency "Tune In Digital", I worked with the Lions Eye Institute (LEI) to develop marketing & communications strategies, both online and offline.

As well as planning and advisory support, I led the design team in the creation and implementation of our products, working alongside LEI's Marketing Advisor. The outcome was a well rounded campaign designed to appeal directly to their market, utilising radio advertising on the Austereo network as well as Nova, driving traffic to a website specifically tailored to their audience. It involved pitching the campaign to LEI, working directly with LEI and Radio stations and consultation with their creative teams. The ‘See the world through your own eyes’ campaign continues to flourish today, generating outstanding results for LEI, converting leads from 29% of all unique visitors to the website.
